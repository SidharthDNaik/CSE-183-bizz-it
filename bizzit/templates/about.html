About Bizzit

Bizzit creates a space for small businesses in Santa Cruz to promote their businesses and allows the community to support them. Due to COVID-19 several corporations struggled keeping up their businesses, but the impact of COVID-19 on smaller businesses without the same marketing capacity and ability was exponentially worse. Many businesses had no choice but to close their doors. Our hope is that Bizzit can help small businesses attract more customers during this time by utilizing a site exclusively for small businesses.
